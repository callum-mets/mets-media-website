How We Work

Brief to Final Cut.
No Surprises.

A straight-line process built for site schedules and procurement timelines.

  1. 01

    Same week

    Brief

    A 30-minute call to understand the goal, audience, and deliverables. You'll have a written quote in your inbox within 24 hours.

  2. 02

    1–2 weeks lead

    Pre-Production

    Site recce, shot list, schedule, and access plan. We work around your operations — never the other way around.

  3. 03

    1–3 days typical

    Shoot

    On-site capture with full kit — cameras, drone, lighting, audio. We slot into your rotation. Daily reviews on request.

  4. 04

    2–3 weeks turnaround

    Edit and colour in DaVinci Resolve. One round of revisions included. Final files in every format you need — broadcast, web, social, OOH.
