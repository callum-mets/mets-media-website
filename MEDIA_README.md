# Open Graph Social Share Image

When the website link is shared on Slack, LinkedIn, WhatsApp, Instagram DM, etc, this image is what shows up in the preview card.

| File | Path | Specs |
|------|------|-------|
| og-image.jpg | images/og/ | 1200x630 JPG, under 500 KB |

## What to put on it

- A strong single hero shot from your work (mining drone shot, equipment in motion, etc.)
- Mets Media logo overlaid in one corner (top-left or bottom-left, cream-coloured)
- Optional: a one-line tagline like "Mining-grade media. Made by people who've worked the mines." in cream over a dark gradient
- Avoid: small text (won't be readable at thumbnail size), busy compositions, more than 2–3 elements

## Why 1200x630

That's the standard size LinkedIn, Facebook, Slack, and most other platforms use for big preview cards. Smaller images get rejected or look pixelated.

## Test before launch

After uploading, paste the live URL into:
- https://www.opengraph.xyz/ (free OG preview tester)
- https://www.linkedin.com/post-inspector/ (LinkedIn-specific)

Both will show you exactly how the preview renders.
